Draft Weight

This year there is a change in how draft weights will be determined. Previously, the order of finish determined the draft weight. The new system incorporates three factors; the order of finish, last year's deviation and the number of losses. Below you will find each team's possible weight for each of these three factors, as well as the total range. Remember if a team has a final weight of 0 or negative they will automaticlly be placed in the final draft position. These tables indicate each team's draft weight for the 2000 season.

Previous deviation is the position that the team drafted in this year minus the position they were expected to draft in based on their weight.
