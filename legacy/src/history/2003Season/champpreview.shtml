Preview For Championship

#1 War Eagles (12-1-2) vs #3 Illuminati (9-5-1)

  • War Eagles won the meeting in week 1, 19-10
  • They tied in week 7, 30-30
  • All-time the Illuminati lead the series 15-5-1
  • Illuminati have not defeated War Eagles since week 2 of 2002
  • Neither team has ever won a Championship
  • Last time neither Championship game participant had won a previous title was 1996 (Norsemen vs ZEN)
  • War Eagles (then the Archers) lost the 1992 Championship to the Renegades (now the Crusaders)
  • Illuminati (then Tsunami) lost the 1993 and 1995 Championships, both to the Barbarians (now Whiskey Tango)
  • Illuminati are 3-7 all-time in the post-season
  • War Eagles are 1-1 all-time in the playoffs, 1-3 when Toliet Bowls are factored in
  • Current 5 game winning streak by the War Eagles is tied for longest in franchise history
  • Illuminati's win last week broke a five game playoff losing streak for them
  • The line on this game is even
