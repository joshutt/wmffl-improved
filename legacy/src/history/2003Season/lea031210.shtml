Award Nominations

As we did the past couple of years I am once again soliciting nominations for the three voted on ownership awards; The Bonehead Award, Best Ownership Move and Overall Best Owner. The Bonehead Award goes to the team that made the stupidest move or moves that hurt that team's own chances of success. The Best Ownership Move is awarded to the single best move that improved a team's success. Finally, the Overall Best Owner goes to the person that performed the best in all aspects of the game, for this award everything can and should be considered including, what the team started with, how the team finished, the quality of drafting and transactions, etc.

I ask that everyone consider which owners they think should awarded each of these awards. Send me an email with you nominations and reasons for the nomination. Next week the voting will take place.
