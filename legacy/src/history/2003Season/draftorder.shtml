August 10, 2003

This is the offical draft order for the 2003 Draft to be held at Mike's house on August 24 at 1:00. Also remember that your protections are due by Sunday, August 17th. The order was determined based on a lottery of non-playoff teams. The weights were: War Eagles - 6; Rednecks - 5; Whiskey Tango - 4; Crusaders - 3; Green Wave - 2; and Freezer Burn - 1.
