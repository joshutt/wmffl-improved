2003 Week 1


WMFFL Newsletter Week #1
Home Page: http://wmffl.com
=======================================================

Week 1 proved to be an interesting week.  The MeggaMen extended their 
regular season winning streak to 8 games, now tied for the fourth 
longest in league history.  The Werewolves managed to defeat the 
Norsemen for only the the second time in their last 11 meetings.  The 
War Eagles surprised the Illuminati, giving that franchise it's first 
opening day loss in five years.  The Whiskey Tango picked up their 
first win under the new name downing the Crusaders by 12.  Finally, 
the Rednecks and Freezer Burn met in a defensive struggle and ended in
 a 0-0 tie.

There are still a couple of teams, Illuminati and Whiskey Tango, that 
have not turned in thier league entry fee.  The Illuminati owe $9.39 
and the Whiskey Tango owe $48.12.
