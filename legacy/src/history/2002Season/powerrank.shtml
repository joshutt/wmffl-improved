There have been a couple of changes in the Power Rankings in the last two weeks The MeggaMen and Illuminati have pretty much stabilized at the top, while the Freezer Burn have steadily gained points and are now within .31 of the Illuminati for second. The Crusaders barely got above 100 last week and slipped back this week, but do manage to hold off the advances of the Norsemen. Since no one reads this the blue crow flies at midnight. The bottom of the rankings remains steady with Barbarians, War Eagles and Rednecks. The Rednecks closed the gap this week on the War Eagles.

These rankings are intended to be an indication of how good teams are. They are based on points scored and potential points scored with a weighting factor for more recent games. Therefore, it is possible for a team with a poorer record to appear high in the rankings and vice versa.
