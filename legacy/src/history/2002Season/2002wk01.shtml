2002 Week 1


WMFFL Newsletter Week #1
Home Page: http://wmffl.com
=======================================================

Finally, the new season has started.  The Green Wave, Illuminati and 
War Eagles all win convincingly.  Helped out by missing activations, 
the MeggaMen record a solid victory.  While, the Werewolves edge the 
Rednecks.  

The Norsemen failed to activate 6 players and so recieved a $6 fine 
and 12 point penalty.  The Barbarians missed 8 players , costing them
$8 and 16 points.  Also the Illuminati have yet to pay their entry 
fee and should be subject to ridicule and public humiliation.
