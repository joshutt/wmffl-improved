2002 Award Nominees

December 18, 2002

I have recieved a number of nominations for each of the awards. The field has been narrowed down to three for each category. I have also included nominees for MVP and Best Defensive Player. The player awards are based on number of points acored, above the opponent at the same position.

I would appreciate it if everyone could vote for one in each category and send your votes to me by Sunday. The winners will be announced next week. Thanks for everyone that submitted thier ideas.
