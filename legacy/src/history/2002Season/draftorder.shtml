Draft Order

August 17, 2002

This is the offical draft order for the 2002 Draft to be held at Jim's house on August 18 at 1:00. Also remember that your protections are due by Sunday, August 11th. The order was determined based on a lottery of non-playoff teams. The weights were: Rednecks - 6; MeggaMen - 5; Barbarians - 4; Green Wave - 3; Norsemen - 2; and War Eagles - 1.
