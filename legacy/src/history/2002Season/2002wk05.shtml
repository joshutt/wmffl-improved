WMFFL Newsletter Week #5
Home Page: http://wmffl.com
=======================================================
The Norsemen activated the Minnesota Offensive Line, who were on a 
bye.  So they get penalized 2 points and $1.

The Rednecks/ZEN regular season losing streak of 10 games is tied for
the 2nd longest in league history.  They did win the Toliet Bowl last
year, but have not won a regular season game since week 9 of last 
seaosn.

The MeggaMen's 5 game winning streak is tied for the longest in 
frachise history.  They also won 5 games between weeks 5 and 9 of the
2000 season.
