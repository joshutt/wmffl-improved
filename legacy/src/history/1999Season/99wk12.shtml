Playoff Possiblities
====================
  Archers Who Say Ni - Eliminated from playoff contention
  Barbarians         - Make playoffs if they win both remaining games AND
                       Werewolves lose both remaining games.
  Crusaders          - Have clinched playoff berth and division title
  Green Wave         - Eliminated from playoff contention
  Hempaholics        - Eliminated from playoff contention
  Norsemen           - Clinched playoff berth
                       Can win division with win over ZEN OR
                       Win over Werewolves and ZEN lose to Barbarians OR
                       Tie vs ZEN and same or better vs Werewolves as ZEN 
                         vs Barbarians
  Werewolves         - Make playoffs with a win or tie OR
                       Barbarians lose or tie
  ZEN                - Clinched playoff berth
                       Can win division with win over Norsemen and win or 
                         tie vs Barbarians OR
                       win over Norsemen and Norsemen lose to Werewolves OR
                       tie vs Norsemen, win or tie vs Barbarians and  
                         Norsemen lose or tie Werewolves

Toliet Bowl Possibilities
=========================
  Archers Who Say Ni - Makes Toliet Bowl with 1 lose or tie OR
                       Hempaholics and Green Wave each winning or tying 1 game
  Barbarians         - Makes Toliet Bowl with 1 loss or tie OR
                       Werewolves win or tie 1 game
  Crusaders          - Eliminated from Toliet Bowl consideration
  Green Wave         - Forces a tie with 2 losses and Archers win twice
  Hempaholics        - Forces a tie with 2 losses and Archers win twice
  Norsemen           - Eliminated from Toliet Bowl consideration
  Werewolves         - Make Toliet Bowl with 2 losses and Barbarians win twice
  ZEN                - Eliminated from Toliet Bowl consideration
