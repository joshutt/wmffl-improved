Wolverine Bowl VIII
==========================================
#1 Norsemen (12-3) vs #3 ZEN (11-4)
   Norsemen are Blue Division Champions
   The Norsemen beat ZEN in 1996 Championship, 15-0
   That was both team's only trips to the Championship
   Norsemen swept season series
   Norsemen lead all-time series 8-6
   Norsemen have won last 3 head-to-head
   A Norsemen win will be 8th straight win (ties team record
      and tied 2nd best in league history)
   ZEN broke 3 game playoff losing streak with win over
      Crusaders (league record)
   No team has loss to the Barbarians and won the Championship
      in the same season.  Both the Norsemen and ZEN lost to
      the Barbarians this season.
   In the each of the previous 7 championships, the team with
      the better record won the game
   Norsemen are 10 1/2 point favorites
