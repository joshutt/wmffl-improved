Iradicators become
the Green Wave

August 6, 1999

The Iradicators announced to today that they are changing their name to the Green Wave. The owner of the Green Wave, Ira Frankel, made the following statement;

"The Iradicators have changed their team name and colors. It just took a while to clear it with the city but, the vote passed. The new team name is now the GREEN WAVE and the team colors are green and black and silver."

The Green Wave become the first team to change their name in two years. They have been known as the Iradicators since 1995. Before that they had been called the Cloakers and the Zebus.
