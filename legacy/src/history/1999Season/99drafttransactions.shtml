1999 Pre-Draft Transactions

August 14, 1999
  • League
    • Teams stocked with drafted players. (See draft picks)
  • Barbarians
    • Fired Head Coach Gunther Cunningham (KC) and hired Dennis Green (HC-MIN)
  • Hempaholics
    • Traded Atlanta Offensive Line to Norsemen in exchange for Tennessee offensive line.
  • Norsemen
    • Traded Tennessee Offensive line to the Hempaholics in exchange for Atlanta Offensive Line.
  • ZEN
    • Fired Head Coach George Seifert (CAR) and hired Tony Dungy (HC-TB)
August 13, 1999
  • Crusaders
    • Fired Head Coach Ray Rhodes (GB) and hired Bill Parcells (HC-NYJ)
August 10, 1999
  • Barbarians
    • Named a 12th round pick as future considerations to complete trade with Norsemen.
  • Norsemen
    • Fired Head Coach Bill Cowher (PIT) and hired Mike Shannahan (HC-DEN)
    • Accepted a 12th round pick from Barbarians to complete previous trade
August 7, 1999
  • Barbarians
    • Traded future considerations to Norsemen in exchange for Marvcus Patton (LB-KC), Jason Belser (DB-IND) and Deion Sanders (DB-DAL)
  • Norsemen
    • Traded Marvcus Patton (LB-KC), Jason Belser (DB-IND) and Deion Sanders (DB-DAL) to Barbarians in exchange for future considerations.
August 6, 1999
  • Green Wave
    • Team announced renaming to Green Wave, from Iradicators
    • Traded NY Jets QB Unit to ZEN in exchange for Antonio Langham (DB-CLE)
  • Hempaholics
    • Traded extra WR protection (obtained from ZEN) back to ZEN in exchange for a swap of draft picks in the 1st and 3rd rounds.
  • ZEN
    • Traded Antonio Langham (DB-CLE) to Green Wave in exchange for the NY Jets QB Unit.
    • Swapped 1st and 3rd Round picks with Hempaholics in exchange for a WR protection.
