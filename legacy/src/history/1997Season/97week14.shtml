REMINDER TO PLAYOFF TEAMS
====================================================
There is a game on Thursday this week, so activations are due by
then.  In addition, when you submit your activations, you must
number your players 1 through 14 (do not include the head coach)
in any order that you want.  In the event of a tie, the #1 players
will be compared, then #2 etc, until the tie is broken.  If you do
not number the players then you will automaticly lose any tie games.
