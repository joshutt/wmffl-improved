Owner of the Year
The Owner of the Year Award is given to the team who produced the best overall managed team. This is determined based on three factors; 1) How well players are obtained, 2) How well the players on roster are used and 3) The overall result (record, with a bonus for playoff performance). The order of finish is shown below.

2.Hempaholics
