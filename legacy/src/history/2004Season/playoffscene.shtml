Playoff Scenarios

Division Championbships

  • Norsemen - Have clinched Burgandy division title, can clinch #1 seed with win vs Rednecks
  • Rednecks - Can clinch Gold division title with a win OR a Whiskey Tango loss or tie, can clinch #1 seed with win vs Norsemen OR a tie and a Whiskey Tango loss or tie
  • Whiskey Tango - Can clinch Gold division title with a win and a Redneck loss or tie OR a tie and a Redneck loss, can clinch #1 seed with a win and a Norsemen and Redneck tie

Wild Card

  • MeggaMen - Clinch a wild card spot with a win and either a Werewolves loss or tie or a Whiskey Tango loss OR a Werewolves loss and either a tie or a Bug Stompers win
  • Rednecks - Have clinched at least a wild card spot
  • Werewolves - Can clinch a wild card with a win OR a tie and a MeggaMen loss or tie OR both MeggaMen loss and Bug Stompers loss or tie
  • Whiskey Tango - Can clinch a playoff spot with a win or tie or MeggaMen loss or tie or Werewolves loss or tie

Toliet Bowl

  • Crusaders - clinch Toliet Bowl berth with loss or tie or Gallic Warriors win or tie
  • Gallic Warriors - clinch Toliet Bowl berth with loss and Crusaders win
  • Illuminati - clinch Toliet Bowl berth with loss and Sacks on the Beach win or tie OR a tie and Sacks on the Beach win
  • Sacks on the Beach - clinch a Toliet Bowl berth with a loss or Illuminati win or both tie

Eliminated From Everything

  • Bug Stompers - Fourth straight year the defending champions failed to make the playoffs.
