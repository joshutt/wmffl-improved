2004 Week 2


WMFFL Newsletter Week #2
Home Page: http://wmffl.com
=======================================================
For the second week in a row, the Norsemen found themselves trailing 
by quite a bit headed into Monday night.  This time however, they are
unable to complete the comeback and fall to the MeggaMen 31-28.  The 
Crusaders also lost a close one, losing to Whiskey Tango 30-28.  

The Rednecks ended the 7 game winning streak of the Bug Stompers with
a 16-6 victory.  The Werewolves trample to Illuminati 31-0 to move to
2-0.  The Gallic Warriors pick up their first win under new ownership,
defeating the Sacks on the Beach, 66-43.
