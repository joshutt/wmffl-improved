Draft Weight and Schedule

The draft order is determined b reverse order of finish, with a lottery among the non-playoff teams for the first three slots. The weights for the lottery will be based on the order of finish. The draft order will remain the same throughout the draft. Shown below is a table of every team's possible draft positions and lottery weight.

In addition to the draft order, the schedule is partially based on order of finish. Every team plays every other team in their division 2 times and every team in the opposing division once. However, that only leaves 13 games. The remaining game comes from playing one team in the opposing division a second time.
