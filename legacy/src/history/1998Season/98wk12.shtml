Playoff Picture
=====================================

Have Clinched Playoff Berths
Crusaders  (*5th franchise appearance, 3rd consecutive)
Norsemen   (3rd franchise appearance)
Werewolves (*5th franchise appearance, 2nd consecutive)

* - sets league record


Have Clinched Division Title
Crusaders - Orange Division (2nd franchise division title)


To Clinch Playoff Berth

ZEN -
   Win or Tie vs either Norsemen or Werewolves OR
   Barbarians lose to or tie either Archers Who Say Ni or Iradicators
Barbarians -
   Win vs Archers Who Say Ni and Iradicators AND
   ZEN lose to Norsemen and Werewolves.


To win Blue Division

ZEN -
   Win vs. Norsemen and Werewolves AND
   Werewolves lose to Hempaholics AND
   One of the following occurs:
     Norsemen beat Archers Who Say Ni OR
     Outscore Werewolves by 45 points

Norsemen -
   Win vs. ZEN and Archers Who Say Ni and Werewolves lose 1 or
     tie both games OR
   Win vs. ZEN and tie Archers Who Say Ni and Werewolves lose 1 and
     tie the other OR
   Win vs. ZEN and Werewolves lose to Hempaholics and ZEN OR
   Tie vs. ZEN and Win vs. Archers Who Say Ni and Werewolves lose 1
     and tie or lose other

Werewolves -
   Win vs. Hempaholics or ZEN and tie or win other game OR
   Win vs. Hempaholics or ZEN and Norsemen lose or tie to either ZEN
     or Archers Who Say Ni OR
   Tie both Hempaholics and ZEN and Norsemen lose or tie to either ZEN
     or Archers Who Say Ni OR
   Tie vs. Hempaholics or ZEN and Norsemen lose to either ZEN or
     Archers Who Say Ni OR
   Tie vs. Hempaholics or ZEN and Norsemen tie both ZEN and
     Archers Who Say Ni OR
   ZEN beat Norsemen and Norsemen lose to or tie Archers Who Say Ni
     and ZEN doesn't outscore by 45 points OR
   ZEN and Norsemen tie and Norsemen lose to Archers Who Say Ni.

Other possiblities exist, but are highly unlikely.
