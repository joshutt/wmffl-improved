Playoff Picture
=============================

* - sets league record


Have Clinched Division Title
Crusaders - Orange Division (2nd franchise division title)


To Clinch Playoff Berth

ZEN -         Win or Tie vs Werewolves OR
              Barbarians lose or tie to Iradicators
Barbarians -  Win vs Iradicators AND
              ZEN lose Werewolves.


To win Blue Division and Number 1 Seed

Norsemen -    Win vs. Archers Who Say Ni AND
              Werewolves lose to ZEN
Werewolves -  Win or tie vs. ZEN OR
              Norsemen lose or tie to Archers Who Say Ni


Seeds

Crusaders  -  Locked into Number 2 Seed
Norsemen   -  Win Number 1 Seed if they win division else Number 3
Werewolves -  Win Number 1 Seed if they win division else Number 3
ZEN        -  If they make the playoffs will be Number 4 Seed
