WMFFL Newsletter Week #1
Home Page: http://wmffl.com
=======================================================
Sorry, for the delay in getting this out.  With everything
that has unfolded in the last few hours football seems very
insignificant.  I know that many of us have loved ones that
work in some of the locations that were attacked this morning.
Everyone offers thier prayers for thier saftey and for all
other victims of these heinous acts.  God Bless America!!

The Norsemen failed to submit a full line-up and therefore
were fined $1 for an illegal lineup.  ZEN activated a player
on a bye so will be fined an additional $1.  A number of teams
have not paid their entry fees and have had a $1 and $5 fee
added to them.  If you need to know how much you owe, let me
know.
