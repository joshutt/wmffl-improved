Draft Weight

This is the second year our new draft weight system has been in place. The new system incorporates three factors; the order of finish, last year's deviation and the number of losses. Below you will find each team's possible weight for each of these three factors, as well as the total range. Remember if a team has a final weight of 0 or negative they will automaticlly be placed in the final draft position. These tables indicate each team's draft weight for the 2001 draft.

Previous Deviation
Previous deviation is the position that the team drafted in this year minus the position they were expected to draft in based on their weight.
