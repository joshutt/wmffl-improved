Named Quarterbacks

Last Updated 1/24/2002

As we convert from team Quarterbacks to individual Quarterbacks everyone is expected to pick a quarterback from each team whose QB they have to make the individual QB that they have on roster. Below you will find a list for each team with the QB unit that they currently have and either the individual QB that they picked or the default one, which will take that position if the owner fails to change it. So if you don't like the individual QB that you have, change it before it is too late. The deadline to change is the Super Bowl, so February 3, 2002.
