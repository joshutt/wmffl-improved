Scoring Correction From Week #3
=================================================================
  The NFL has announced a scoring change from week 3.  One of
Brett Farve's interceptions was changed to a fumble by the NFL
after the game film was reviewed.  So he should have 1 interception
and 1 fumble (instead of 2 interceptions and 0 fumbles).  And
Willie Clark of San Diego should have a fumble recovery instead of
an interception.  The appropriate changes have been made in this
week's report.
