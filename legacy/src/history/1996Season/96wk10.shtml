Notes From the Commissioner
===============================
 Tie Breakers
Some people have asked to be reminded on what the procedures for
tie-breaking are, should two teams end up with the same record.  Here
they are:
  1. Head-to-Head record
  2. Total Points scored
  3. Total Points in Head-To-Head Games
  4. Average Points by Head Coach
  5. Fantsy Ranking
  6. Historical Tendancy
There has never been a tie that was not broken by the 2nd tie-breaker,
so numbers 3 through 6 are pretty much irrelevant, but just in case.
