12 teams
posted by Mike Atlas, Sacks on the Beach Owner at 2:39 PM

I would be in favor of an expansion to 12 teams. However, my only fear would be that the teams would get too watered down (who is the Lions' slot receiver again?"). In order to stop that a little, I would want to reduce the number of total roster slots to 23. That would, in turn, only add four extra slots to the league, and force us to manage those fewer sports better.
