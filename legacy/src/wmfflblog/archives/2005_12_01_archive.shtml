Why Ayrik Should be Pissed at Hall
posted by Josh Utterback, Werewolves Owner at 11:09 PM

A bit of a flash back to last season when the reinstatement of the 1-0 rule came up. Hall claimed to be adamantly opposed to it, firm and unyielding. This season he changes his colors and allows the rule to pass.

Fast forward a bit to week 3 of this year. The MeggaMen are struggling, they had been shutout in both of the first two games. They get shutout again by the Norsemen, however because of the horrendous 1-0 rule they get a win. A win that should have been a 0-0 tie.

Back to the present. Five teams finish 9-5, one of them is the MeggaMen. Without the 1-0 rule, they would have been 8-5-1 and out of the playoffs. The Bug Stompers would have been in, in their place. (It would have been nice irony if it was the Crusaders that got screwed, but it didn't work out that way).

I say that if the MeggaMen repeat as Champions we put an asterisk next to their name.
